# Gameplay Testing & QA Analysis Report  
## Bright Memory: Infinite

---

# 1. Game Overview

**Genre:** First-Person Shooter / Action  
**Developer:** FYQD Studio  
**Publisher:** PLAYISM  

**Primary Focus:** Fast-paced combat, combo-based gameplay, shooting mechanics, melee combat, mobility systems, and cinematic visual presentation.

*Bright Memory: Infinite* is a fast-paced FPS action game that combines:

- Gun combat  
- Sword attacks  
- Mobility mechanics  
- Special abilities  
- Combo systems  

into a stylish and aggressive combat experience. The game focuses heavily on cinematic action, fluid movement, and visually intense combat encounters.

---

# 2. Core Gameplay Loop Analysis

The primary gameplay loop includes:

1. Fighting enemy groups  
2. Combining gunplay with melee attacks  
3. Using mobility and special abilities  
4. Progressing through linear combat sections  
5. Upgrading combat flow through skill usage  
6. Defeating mini-bosses and boss encounters  

The gameplay loop remains engaging because:

- Combat is fast and visually exciting  
- Movement feels aggressive and fluid  
- Combo chaining creates satisfying combat flow  

The game focuses more on action intensity and cinematic combat than exploration or survival mechanics.

---

# 3. Combat System Evaluation

## Strengths

### Fast-Paced Combat

The combination of:

- Shooting  
- Sword combat  
- Air combos  
- Mobility abilities  

creates highly energetic combat encounters.

### Combo Potential

Players can chain attacks creatively using:

- Launch abilities  
- Melee attacks  
- Ranged weapons  
- Enemy juggling mechanics  

### Movement & Mobility

Sliding, dashing, and aerial movement improve combat pacing and make encounters feel dynamic.

### Visual Feedback

Particle effects, animations, and enemy reactions help attacks feel impactful and stylish.

### Weapon Variety

Different weapons and abilities support multiple combat approaches and improve gameplay variety.

---

## Weaknesses & Issues

### Personal Gameplay Findings

#### Enemy Variety Limitations

Some combat encounters begin to feel repetitive because enemy types and attack behaviors remain limited throughout progression.

#### Camera Disorientation

During fast melee combat and aerial combos, camera movement can occasionally become difficult to track.

#### Short Campaign Length

The overall gameplay experience feels shorter compared to many modern action-focused games.

---

### Community-Reported Issues

Players on Reddit, Steam reviews, and community discussions frequently mentioned:

#### Difficulty Spikes

Many players reported sudden difficulty increases during certain boss fights and enemy-heavy encounters.

#### Enemy AI Simplicity

Community feedback often mentioned that enemies:

- Rush directly toward the player  
- React slowly in some situations  
- Lack advanced combat behavior  

#### Limited Replay Value

Some players felt the game lacks enough additional modes, progression depth, or content for long-term replayability.

---

# 4. Level Design & Progression Analysis

## Strengths

### Cinematic Presentation

Environmental visuals and scripted action moments help maintain excitement throughout gameplay progression.

### Strong Gameplay Pacing

The game keeps players engaged by quickly transitioning between combat encounters and cinematic moments.

### Clear Objective Design

Linear progression keeps navigation simple and avoids unnecessary confusion.

---

## Weaknesses & Issues

### Personal Gameplay Findings

#### Limited Exploration

Most levels focus heavily on combat progression with minimal exploration opportunities.

#### Low Environmental Interaction

Environments look visually impressive but offer limited gameplay interaction outside combat sequences.

---

### Community-Reported Issues

#### Level Similarity

Some players reported that several combat areas begin to feel visually similar during longer sessions.

#### Lack of Additional Content

Community discussions frequently mentioned limited:

- Endgame systems  
- Replay incentives  
- Additional gameplay modes  

---

# 5. UI / UX Evaluation

## Strengths

- HUD remains clean during combat  
- Ability cooldown indicators are easy to track  
- Weapon switching feels responsive and smooth  

---

## Weaknesses & Issues

### Personal Gameplay Findings

#### Visual Overload

Heavy particle effects and combat visuals can occasionally reduce visibility during larger encounters.

---

### Community-Reported Issues

#### UI Scaling Problems

Some PC players reported:

- Incorrect UI scaling  
- Overlapping HUD elements  
- Resolution-related display issues  

#### Tutorial Clarity

A few players mentioned that advanced combat mechanics could be explained more clearly during early gameplay.

---

# 6. Audio & Visual Feedback Analysis

## Strengths

### Visual Effects

Lighting, particle effects, and combat animations strongly improve gameplay presentation.

### Weapon Audio

Gunshots, sword effects, and combat sounds provide satisfying gameplay feedback.

### Environmental Presentation

The game delivers high-quality visuals and strong environmental detail considering its smaller development scale.

---

## Weaknesses & Issues

### Personal Gameplay Findings

#### Combat Readability

Bright visual effects occasionally make enemy attacks harder to read during fast combat encounters.

---

### Community-Reported Issues

#### Audio Mixing Issues

Some players reported:

- Inconsistent voice volume  
- Loud environmental effects  
- Uneven combat sound balancing  

#### Optimization Complaints

Community discussions often mentioned:

- FPS drops  
- Stuttering  
- Performance instability during effects-heavy battles  

---

# 7. Technical & QA Observations

## Personal Gameplay Findings

### Collision Observations

- Some environmental collisions occasionally feel inconsistent during high-speed movement  
- Enemy positioning can sometimes overlap during close-range combat  

### Animation Timing

Certain enemy attack animations occasionally feel abrupt during faster encounters.

---

## Community-Reported Issues

### Performance Stability Problems

Players frequently reported:

- Frame drops during explosions  
- GPU-heavy combat scenes  
- Inconsistent optimization on mid-range hardware  

### Crash Reports

Some community feedback included:

- Random crashes  
- Freezing during cutscenes  
- Loading-related stability issues  

### Input Detection Complaints

A few players discussed delayed input responses during fast combo chains and ability usage.

---

# 8. Difficulty & Balance Evaluation

The game generally maintains exciting combat pacing through:

- Fast enemy encounters  
- Mobility-focused gameplay  
- Combo-based combat systems  

Combat becomes most enjoyable when players combine:

- Movement  
- Melee attacks  
- Abilities  
- Gunplay  

together during aggressive encounters.

However:

- Limited enemy variety  
- Sudden difficulty spikes  
- Short progression length  

reduce long-term gameplay depth.

---

# 9. Improvement Suggestions

## Gameplay Improvements

- Add more enemy types and combat variety  
- Expand exploration opportunities and environmental interaction  
- Increase replay systems and post-game content  

---

## Combat Improvements

- Improve combat readability during effects-heavy encounters  
- Balance sudden difficulty spikes during boss fights  
- Improve enemy combat behavior variety  

---

## Technical Improvements

- Further optimize performance during large combat sequences  
- Improve input consistency during fast combo gameplay  
- Reduce collision inconsistencies during movement  

---

# 10. Final Evaluation

*Bright Memory: Infinite* delivers a visually impressive and fast-paced action experience through:

- Stylish combat systems  
- Strong visual presentation  
- Satisfying combo mechanics  
- High gameplay intensity  

The game performs best during aggressive combat encounters where players creatively combine shooting, melee combat, abilities, and mobility mechanics.

Although short campaign length, limited enemy variety, and optimization issues occasionally affect the experience, the overall combat system remains highly entertaining and mechanically satisfying.
